Chapter 1 About

This is a manual of operations for the Agios Informatics team. Its puspose is to:

  • provide a resource on best practices (Chapter 2)
  • aid in setting up effective and reproducible project workflows (Chapter 3)
  • promote learning and sharing of ideas (Chapter 4).
